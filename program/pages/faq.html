General

MoneyFirst is a personal budget tracker that helps you manage your income, expenses, savings goals, and monthly bills — all in one place. Your data is securely stored and synced across devices using your account.

Transactions

On the Dashboard, click the + button in the Balance card. A modal will appear where you can enter either an income or an expense. Fill in the amount, description, and any optional notes, then click Add Transaction.

Currently, transactions cannot be edited after they are saved. To correct a mistake, delete the transaction using the ✕ button in the History tab and add a new one with the correct details.

Go to the History tab on the Dashboard and click the Clear All button. You will be asked to confirm before anything is deleted. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Savings Goals

Savings goals let you track progress toward a target amount. Create a goal with a name and target, then either manually add savings to it or allocate a percentage of your balance automatically using the Split Ratio feature.

A green bar shows progress toward your goal. When you reach 100%, the bar turns gold to celebrate that your goal is complete!

Split Ratios

Split ratios define how your remaining balance (after fixed bills) is divided between spending categories and savings goals. For example, you might allocate 40% to Entertainment and 60% to Food from your discretionary income.

Click the Edit % button in the Budget Breakdown card on the Dashboard, or navigate to the Split Ratios page directly. There you can configure percentage categories, fixed monthly bills, and savings goal allocations.

Family Portal

Parent accounts can create family spaces, manage invite codes, and view linked child activity in Parent Portal. Child accounts can join a family using an invite code and manage their own budgeting data.

A parent can share their invite code from the Account page. During sign-up (or from Family Portal settings), the child enters that code to connect to the family group.
